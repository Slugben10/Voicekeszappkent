In this interview, a grandchild asks their grandfather about his childhood. The grandfather reminisces about simple playtime activities, stating that he and his friends played outdoor games like football, roller-skating, and catch, as they didn't have access to much equipment. He fondly recalls the delicious home-cooked meals made by his mother, primarily using fruits and vegetables grown in their garden. Lastly, he mentions his childhood friends, highlighting two groups: the neighborhood children he played with and his school friends, expressing a desire to reconnect with them. The interview concludes with gratitude from the grandchild.
